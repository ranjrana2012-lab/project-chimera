## Technical Innovation

### Core Achievement: Adaptive AI Based on Sentiment

**Unlike traditional chatbots** that use the same response regardless of emotional state, Project Chimera **adapts its dialogue strategy, tone, and context** based on detected sentiment.

**Three Adaptive Strategies**:
1. **Positive Sentiment** → "momentum_build" strategy with enthusiastic tone
2. **Negative Sentiment** → "supportive_care" strategy with empathetic tone
3. **Neutral Sentiment** → "standard_response" strategy with professional tone

**Demonstrated Value**:
- Clear difference between adaptive and non-adaptive modes
- Real-time sentiment detection (<500ms)
- Appropriate tone matching
- Context-aware responses
