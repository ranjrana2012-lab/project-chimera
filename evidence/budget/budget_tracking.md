## Notes

### Budget Justification

All expenditures must be:
1. ✅ Directly related to project deliverables
2. ✅ Reasonable and necessary
3. ✅ Properly documented
4. ✅ Approved by project lead

### Receipt Storage

**Physical Receipts**: Store in `evidence/budget/receipts/`
**Digital Receipts**: Save as PDF in same directory

**Naming Convention**: `YYYY-MM-DD_VENDOR_DESCRIPTION.pdf`

Example: `2026-04-09_Amazon_DGX_Server.pdf`

### Invoice Submission

**When**: All invoices collected by Week 6
**Where**: `evidence/budget/` directory
**Format**: PDF or high-quality scan
**Metadata**: Include in budget_tracking.md
