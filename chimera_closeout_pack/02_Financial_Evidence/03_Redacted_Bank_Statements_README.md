# Redacted Bank Statements Guidance

Status: TEMPLATE - HUMAN ACTION REQUIRED.

Codex cannot verify that funds left the account unless bank statements are
present and reviewed by a human. Do not upload unnecessary personal data to
public git.

## Statements Must Show

- Account holder or business identifier sufficient for reviewer confirmation.
- Transaction date.
- Payee / supplier name.
- Amount paid.
- Reference matching the invoice or evidence index where available.

## May Be Redacted

- Unrelated transactions.
- Full account number beyond what the funder requires.
- Personal spending unrelated to Project Chimera.
- Personal addresses if not required by the funder.

## Must Not Be Redacted

- The transaction line being claimed.
- Payee / supplier for the claimed transaction.
- Date and amount for the claimed transaction.
- Any reference needed to match the invoice and spending summary.

Store redacted statements privately and reference them in
`02_Evidence_Reference_Index_TEMPLATE.csv`.
