## Strategic Pivot Decision

**Date**: Week 5 (March 2026)
**Decision**: Transition from distributed team to solo development
**Rationale**: Evidence-based decision making

### Reasons for Pivot
1. **Student Engagement**: Sprint 0 dormant 5+ weeks with 0 PRs
2. **Technical Complexity**: Microservices too complex for 8-week timeline

## Lessons Learned

### What Didn't Work
1. **Student-Led Model**: Required more time and infrastructure than available
2. **Volunteer-Based Development**: No incentives or accountability
3. **Complex Onboarding**: Kubernetes/Docker too steep for 8-week timeline
4. **No Venue Partnership**: Hardware integration impossible without venue

### Recommendations for Phase 2
1. **Paid Internships**: Consider paid vs. volunteer model
2. **Simplified Onboarding**: Git basics only, pre-configured environments
3. **Venue Partnership**: Secure venue before starting Phase 2
4. **Modular Timeline**: Break into 3-month phases with clear milestones
